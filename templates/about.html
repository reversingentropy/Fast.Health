The Story

Heart diseases are a rising issue in Singapore due to itsâ€™ aging population.
As of 2019, one in three deaths are lost to CVDs (cardiovascular disease). Heart attacks are highly preventable with simple lifestyle modifications such as reducing alcohol and smoking, eating healthily, and exercising often. What is Our Goal

1. To create a machine learning model to predict risk of heart disease based on biological parameters of that person.
2. To develop a web application for the heart disease prediction model where users can input the relevant parameters and retrieve predictions.

Random Forest is a supervised machine learning algorithm made up of decision trees; Random Forest is used for both classification and regression.

One big advantage of random forest is that it can be used for both classification and regression problems, which form the majority of current machine learning systems.
